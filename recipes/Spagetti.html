Spagetti

Ingredients

  • Uncooked Spagetti Noodles
  • Meatballs
  • Basil
  • Marinara Sauce

Instructions

  • Boil Six Cups of Water in a pot
  • Heat Maranaria Sauce and Meatballs in a Sauce pan for 20 minutes
  • Put noodles into pot and stir for 7 minutes
  • Remove the water in a strainer
  • Add the sauce and set to serve
