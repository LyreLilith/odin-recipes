French Toast

Ingredients

  • Four Eggs
  • One Cup of Milk
  • Texas Toast
  • A teapoon of Cinnomon
  • A teaspoon of Vanilla

Instructions

  1. Heat Griddle to 350*F
  2. Mix Eggs, Milk, Cinnomon,and Vanilla
  3. Put toast in the mix on both sides till saturated
  4. Cook on each side for two minutes
  5. Top with your favorite topping
